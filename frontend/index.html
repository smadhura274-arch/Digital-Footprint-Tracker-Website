Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about digital footprint tracking

What is a digital footprint?

A digital footprint is the trail of data you leave behind when using the internet. This includes social media posts, comments, searches, website visits, and any other online activity.

Is my data secure with Digital Footprint Tracker?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never store your personal passwords. Your privacy is our top priority.

How often should I scan my digital footprint?

We recommend scanning at least once a month, but you can schedule automated scans weekly or daily for continuous monitoring.

Can I remove information found by the tracker?

While we can't remove data from third-party sites, we provide detailed instructions and tools to help you request removal or update your privacy settings.

What platforms do you scan?

We scan Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google Search Results, and public databases for your personal information.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes! We offer a free plan with basic scanning features. Premium plans offer advanced analytics and automated monitoring.
